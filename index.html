Welcome to Dataspin Analytics SDK for Unity3D page

Dataspin is an all-in-one back-end solution to manage your game and help you earn more. Tailored for gaming. Lets you communicate with your players better, reward the whales, incentivize, engage, retain. Segment, target them with campaigns, offer items and promos. Manage your store efficiently.

Easy Unity3D integration

Simply import .unitypackage, drop prefab into scene and fire following functions.

Dataspin.Instance.RegisterUser("John", "Dataspin", "john@Dataspin.io");
Dataspin.Instance.RegisterDevice(<notification_id>);
Dataspin.Instance.StartSession();
